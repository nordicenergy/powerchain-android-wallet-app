stablecoin issue and transfer contract

2000 NESC Stablecoins (zu je 1 kWh/Jahr)

This NESC stablecoin Issue and Transfer Agreement (the Agreement) sets out the conditions under which Nordic Energy Corp (Issuer) is deemed to be duly registered under the law of the Republic of Finland under number VAT and is located at the SmartHub - 1st Floor, 65100 Vaasa, Finland , Issue NESC and transfer it to EPIKTET (signatory).

It is therefore agreed as follows:

10% jährliche Tilgung

Der Unterzeichner ist verpflichtet, jährlich mindestens 10% des ursprünglichen Betrages (2000) an den Emittenten zurückzuzahlen, basierend auf einer 100%igen Performance. Die Übertragung kann entweder im CORI-stablecoin or im Ether (ETH) erfolgen. Der Unterzeichner ist berechtigt, einen höheren Betrag zu überweisen. Der Vertrag ist erfüllt, sobald der ursprüngliche Betrag zu 100% an die Emittentin zurückgezahlt wurde.

Monthly Payment

The payments are transferred monthly. The Issuer undertakes to pay the amount due no later than the 10th of each month.

0,0045 ETH For this stablecoin issue and transfer agreement, the Issuer accepts redemptions in NESC stablecoin or in ether. In the event that the transfer takes place in ether, the signatory and the issuer NESC bace value.

For this stablecoin issue and transfer agreement, the Issuer accepts redemptions in NESC stablecoin or in ether. In the event that the transfer takes place in ether, the signatory and the issuer have agreed a fixed exchange rate of 0.0045 ETH to 1 NESC. If both parties agree on the ETH / NESC stablecoin exchange rate, this can be updated with a transaction that introduces a new rate after 3 months.

72h/120h Due Dilligence

The undersigned undertakes to publish the updated ex-post performance data within 72 hours for the duration of the contract. The publisher undertakes to validate the performance data no later than 120 hours after publication. In the event of late delivery of performance data, a warning can be published as a transaction by the issuer. In the event of late delivery of the validation, a warning can be published as a transaction by the signatory.

80%/20% disposal frame

The undersigned ensures ongoing cover of 20% of the NESC stablecoin as a blocked deposit. The undersigned is entitled to transfer / exchange / trade 80% of the open balance (Allowance-To-Value) free of charge.

30 Tage warning period

If the signatory or issuer does not respond to a warning transaction, the sender of the transaction is entitled to collect a penalty fee. The penalty fees are limited to the protected value (see ratio of allowance to value). The withdrawal of a penalty does not cover legal requirements or fines.

6 Months public announcement

The undersigned and the issuer can agree to terminate the obligations under this emission and transfer agreement. The agreement must be published as a transaction by both parties. The contract ends after a notice period of 6 months.
